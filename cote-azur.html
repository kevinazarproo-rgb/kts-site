French Riviera, elegance by the sea

Stretching from Saint-Tropez to Monaco, the French Riviera is the Mediterranean's most prestigious coastline. KTS organises your stay there from start to finish: private yachts, palace hotels, fine dining and privileged access to its major events.

Cities & places

Pearls of the Riviera

Nice

The Promenade des Anglais, the pebble beaches and the baroque old town. KTS arranges private tours of the Cours Saleya market, the Matisse museum and the hills above the bay.

Cannes

La Croisette, the Film Festival and the Lérins islands just offshore. A city of palace hotels and beach clubs, ideal for a glamorous stay or a high-end event.

Monaco

The Rock, the Prince's Palace, the Casino and the Formula 1 Grand Prix. A principality of records where KTS opens the doors to its most exclusive addresses.

Saint-Tropez

Legendary beaches, the fishing port and famous nightlife. From Pampelonne to the village squares, the icon of Riviera summers.

Antibes

Port Vauban and its superyachts, the old town and the Picasso museum. Between Cannes and Nice, a more confidential and authentic Riviera.

Èze

A medieval village perched high above the sea, with its exotic garden and panoramic views. One of the most beautiful viewpoints on the coast.

Experiences

The Riviera art of living

Sea

Yachts & secret coves

A day at sea aboard a private yacht: swimming stops in quiet coves, lunch on board and the coastline seen from the water. We charter the boat, plan the route along the prettiest bays and can arrange a crew and an on-board chef.

Glamour

Festivals & exclusive evenings

Cannes Film Festival, the Monaco Grand Prix, gala dinners: we secure your access to the Riviera's most exclusive events and tables — grandstand seats, private boxes and reservations that are usually out of reach.

Stay

Palaces & private villas

Sea-view suites in the great palace hotels, or a private villa in the hills with dedicated staff and concierge service. We select the address that suits your party and take care of every detail of the stay.
