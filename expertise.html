Our expertise

Three ways to travel with KTS

From intimate tailor-made journeys to large-scale events, KTS brings the same obsession to every project: France, composed by hand.

Group travel

When every detail matters, at any scale.

From a dozen to several hundred guests, KTS designs seamless group journeys across France — every movement choreographed down to the minute.

  • Flawless logistics — transfers, hotels, restaurants and guides coordinated by one dedicated team.
  • Private access — monuments, châteaux and venues opened just for your group.
  • Memorable moments — gala dinners, private concerts and shared experiences no one forgets.
Tailor-made journeys

A journey that exists only for you.

A journey written for you alone — crafted by hand, from the first conversation to the last farewell.

  • We listen — we begin with your desires, your pace and your travellers.
  • We compose — a unique itinerary, refined by our experts and tested in every detail.
  • We watch over everything — a dedicated concierge accompanies you before and throughout.
VIP travel

The exceptional, made effortless.

Confidential access, dedicated concierge and absolute discretion — for those who expect nothing less than the exceptional.

  • The doors without handles — the Palais Garnier after hours, a privatised château, a table with no sign.
  • Private transport — chauffeured cars, private jets and helicopters, to the minute.
  • Absolute discretion — a single point of contact, available around the clock.
