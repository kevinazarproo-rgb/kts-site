Cities & places

Paris and its region

Versailles

The most sumptuous of châteaux: the Hall of Mirrors, the State Apartments and gardens by Le Nôtre. Private visits before opening, dinners in the royal salons and Grandes Eaux evenings on request.

Giverny

Claude Monet's house and gardens, cradle of Impressionism. The water-lily pond and the Japanese bridge, discovered away from the crowds at first light with a private art guide.

Chantilly

A princely château housing the Condé Museum, its Great Stables and a French formal park. Fine dining, a tasting of Chantilly cream and a private equestrian show.

Fontainebleau

From François I to Napoleon, a royal residence nestled in a legendary forest. A thousand years of French history in an intimate setting, far from the crowds.

Reims

An hour from Paris, the cathedral of the kings' coronations and the gateway to Champagne. Prestigious cellars and private tastings at the greatest houses.

Vaux-le-Vicomte

The masterpiece that inspired Versailles. Candlelit evenings in Le Nôtre's gardens and private dinners beneath the gilding of the Grand Salon.

Experiences

Experience Paris differently

Heritage

Heritage & culture

Paris is an exceptional city, rich in unrivalled heritage and culture. With KTS, you discover its finest monuments in private — the Louvre, the Opéra Garnier, the Musée d'Orsay and the Palace of Versailles — with the very best guides.

Fashion

The capital of fashion

The world capital of fashion, Paris is the home of luxury and elegance. KTS opens rare doors for you: haute-couture ateliers, perfume houses and the great jewellers of place Vendôme, where luxury is created.

Gastronomy

The French table

Paris is also a feast for the senses. Legendary Michelin tables, exceptional chefs and great Champagne houses: we craft the city's finest dining experiences for you.

Art de vivre

The art of living à la parisienne

Paris is not a city one ticks off — it is a way of living. Strolling the quais of the Seine, lingering on a café terrace, slipping into a hidden courtyard, dining late in a confidential restaurant.

From the Haussmann boulevards to the village streets of Montmartre and the Marais, every arrondissement has its own character. KTS gives you the keys to the Paris that Parisians keep for themselves.
