Provence, light to share

Lavender fields, sun-drenched markets, stone farmhouses and wine estates: Provence is savoured slowly. KTS designs stays at the pace of the South — between nature, gastronomy and the art of living.

Cities & places

The lands of Provence

Aix-en-Provence

Cours Mirabeau, fountains and Cézanne's studios.

Avignon

The Palais des Papes and the famous Saint-Bénézet bridge.

Gordes

One of the most beautiful hilltop villages of the Luberon.

Arles

Roman arenas and the light so dear to Van Gogh.

Saint-Rémy

Markets, olive groves and ancient heritage.

Les Baux

A city of stone and the spectacular Carrières des Lumières.
